World Building

The action takes place in fiction world between Earth and Swellors' Home World
Because of the stuck in space land, there is a lot of discrepancy between tribes land, sudden changes in weather, hurricanes and country sites. A lot of the Swellors World nature is presented, enormous Highlands, mountain fjords. Used many names from the Bulgarian and slavian Mythology.
